About HoriGene

HoriGene is a bioinformatics web server developed under the Bioclues research consortium, focusing on Horizontal Gene Transfer (HGT) in bacterial pathogens.

Using an AI/ML model based on unsupervised learning on human fungal pathogenesis datasets, HoriGene discovers classification features that predict correlations between pathogenesis and horizontal transfer events.

The portal studies five key gene families — MutS, MutL, RecP, GKI, and XPT — across Streptococcus pyogenes and Lactobacillus strains, revealing patterns of gene presence, absence, and horizontal acquisition.
